# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Zeus welcomes constructive participation from people of every background and experience level.
Contributors must communicate respectfully, give technical feedback in good faith, and keep
discussions focused on improving the project.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate intimidation,
personal attacks, publishing another person's private information, and sustained disruption of
project discussions.

## Scope

This policy applies in the Zeus repository, issue and pull-request discussions, project-managed
community spaces, and public situations where someone is representing Zeus or BrainX.

## Reporting and Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the BrainX maintainers using the contact information on the
[BrainX organization profile](https://github.com/brainx). Do not open a public issue containing
sensitive personal information.

Maintainers will review reports promptly, protect reporters' privacy where practical, and take
proportionate action. Responses may include a private correction, a warning, temporary limits on
participation, or permanent removal from project spaces. Maintainers who are directly involved in
a report must not make the final enforcement decision.

## Good-Faith Reporting

Retaliation against someone who raises a good-faith concern is prohibited. Knowingly false or
malicious reports may themselves result in enforcement action.
